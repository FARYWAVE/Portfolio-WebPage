SBER MOBILE

About

SberMobile is an MVNO (Mobile Virtual Network Operator) launched by Russia's largest financial institution, Sberbank. Operating on the Tele2 network infrastructure, SberMobile integrates deeply within the Sber ecosystem, offering customers unique benefits such as paying up to 99% of their mobile service costs using SberSpasibo loyalty bonuses. The operator provides a full range of services, including voice calls, high-speed 4G/LTE mobile internet, eSIM support, and bundled access to Sber's extensive suite of digital services (e.g., SberBank Online, Okko, SberSound) often with unlimited data access.
